Color Scheme

The color scheme includes: indigo-dye, beige, orange-yellow-crayola, sandy-brown and orange-soda.






Indigo-dye is used in background of the header and footer, the font for the main heading font and navigation tab.



Beige is used in the background of the main body, navigation hover tab, and font for header, footer and active navigation.



Orange-yellow-crayola is for the background of the navigation tab and the rooster's beak in the logo.



Sandy-brown is used for the shadow box of the header.



Orange soda is the background of the navigation active tab, font of the navigation hover and the rooster's comb and waddle in logo.
