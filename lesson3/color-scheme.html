Color Scheme

The color scheme includes: indigo-dye, beige, orange-yellow-crayola, sandy-brown and orange-soda.

Indigo-dye is used in the header and footer background colors, the main heading font and navigation tab font.

Beige is for the background of the main body, background navigation hover tab and font for header and footer and active navigation.

Orange-yellow-crayola is for the background of the navigation tab and the rooster's beak in the logo.

Sandy-brown is used for the shadow box of the header. Orange soda is the background of the navigation active tab, navigation active tab, font of the navigation hover and the rooster's comb and waddle in logo.
