Site Name

The site is going to be called Weather Vane 2 Go. As the name indicates, it will be an online weather vane to check weather and road conditions. Motto: Weather vane to-go in Idaho: come sun, rain, or snow.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of Weather Vane 2 Go will be to get projected weather and road conditions for Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, Idaho.

A secondary objective will be to plan wardrobe and if accessories such as umbrellas are recommended. It can help a person determine if winter gear such as hats and gloves are needed. Maybe it is a day for sandals, sunglasses and sunscreen instead.
